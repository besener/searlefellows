I am an assistant professor of Islamic art and medieval Mediterranean in the Department of Art History.

During the 2025-26 Searle Fellows Program, I met outstanding individuals who generously dedicated their time to teaching, mentoring, and supporting faculty throughout their year-long pedagogical journey. It has been a pleasure to learn not only about their excellent work and experiences but also their philosophies, interests, and favorite readings. I would like to thank the dedicated staff of the Searle Center for Advancing Learning and Teaching: Kate Flom Derrick (Assistant Director of Reflective Pedagogy), Veronica Womack (Associate Director of High-Impact Teaching), Michael Faegan (Searle Fellows Distinguished Faculty Mentor; Professor Emeritus, Preventive Medicine), Jennifer Keys (Senior Director of the Searle Center), Lina Rombalsky Eskew (Senior Assistant Director of Assessment), Denise Drane Senior (Associate Director of Research and Innovation), Laura Ferdinand (Assistant Director of Content and Communications) and Lauri Dietz (former Director of Pedagogy and Curricular Development at Northwestern University and current Executive Director of the Center for Faculty Development at Loyola University of Chicago).
